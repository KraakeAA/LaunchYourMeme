Frequently Asked Questions

We know you have questions. Here are some straightforward answers to the most common ones.

Is this legal and compliant?

Absolutely. We structure every project as a marketing service, focusing on the token's "utility" (like perks and access) rather than as a financial investment. We work diligently to operate within the UK's financial promotion guidelines, ensuring all communications are fair, clear, and include the necessary risk information.

Do my customers need to be crypto experts?

Not at all. We provide simple, step-by-step guides and support to make acquiring and using the token easy for everyone, regardless of their technical knowledge. The focus is on the fun and the community, not the complex technology behind it.

How is this different from a standard points-based loyalty program?

Three key ways: 1) **Ownership:** Customers truly own their tokens, creating a much deeper psychological connection. 2) **Community:** It creates a shared identity and a "club" atmosphere that points can't replicate. 3) **Virality:** The public nature of tokens means every new member and every interaction can be seen, creating organic buzz and social proof that traditional programs lack.
